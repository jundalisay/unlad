---


1. To build a faculty of SCENAC Leaders as Social Entrepreneurs (SE) in the various fields addressing essential needs of the community on: Spiritual and Value Formation, Local Public Administration, Longevity/Health Care and Sanitation, Yoga & Meditation, Entrepreneurship and Inter-trade/Interlink, Family Relations and Family Planning, Emergency and Disaster Management System, Community Defense/Security System and Information Dissemination, and Community Leadership and Personality Development.

2. To build community Kibbutz System (Family Communal Cooperative (FCC)). To help the marginalized and helpless families and individual to work together as one enterprise to support their economic needs and establish Self Reliance Community System, where every family can provide the five (5) basic needs to its members; A community with Health & Wellness Centers where the weak and sickly can avail of free treatment and medication. Provide an opportunity to all members of the community Free Education. Train every family on how to manage their finances and to run its own business as good entrepreneurs.

3. To built a center for Community Network and Information System (CNIS). To provide the SCENAC members information to assist them of their needs covered by the program. Eg. Kibbutz-FCC - to provide access and link to market their products.

4. To be a strong solid voice of the Empowered Coalition of Senior Citizens and PWD sector, patriotic, efficient and competent partner with DSWD, National Senior Citizens Commission, LGU's and other government agencies in the promotion of the general welfare of our society. To help assist the government in fighting graft & corruption, illegal drugs, crimes and terrorism.

5. To initiate programs, projects and social activities on Nutrition, Health and Wellness and Longevity that will involve the Senior Citizens in building an IDEAL SOCIETY as Mentors, Coaches, Advisers and Consultants.
