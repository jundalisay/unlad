## Strategic Objectives

SCENAC aims to offer viable and sustainable Livelihood Programs by implementing practical livelihood projects that can utilize Senior Citizen's inherent talents like caring and nurturing children, listening and counselling, offering solace and comfort to people in need of emotional support, mentoring, coaching and consulting services.

This program will harness the Senior Citizens natural talents, experience and competence and ability and prioritize attention to the needs of the disabled, the sick and the marginalized.
